Introduce vidyasarthi

Vidyasaarathi ( Vidyasaarathi Scholarship Scheme 2023 ) is a technology-enabled initiative by NSDL e-Governance Infrastructure Limited (NSDL e-Gov) to bridge the gap in education finance in the country through an online platform. Students can search and apply for various education finance schemes they are qualified for. Fund providers, industries, and corporates can promote skill development by designing education finance schemes in Vidyasaarathi and manage these schemes.

Vidyasaarathi is a platform by Protean eGov Technologies Limited (Formerly NSDL e-Governance Infrastructure Limited) for applying to various scholarships provided by corporates. Vidyasaarathi does not charge any fees to applicants in any way to apply for scholarships.

Introducing Jansamarth

The Government of India has launched the Jan Samarth platform that connects beneficiaries directly to lenders. It is completely based on credit-linked government schemes. The one-stop digital portal has 13 government credit initiatives on a single platform with 4 loan categories.

The 4 loan categories are education, agricultural infrastructure, business activity and livelihood. The beneficiaries can check their eligibility online and receive digital approval. The objective of the scheme is based on inclusive growth and the development of various sectors by helping them to get the government benefits with simple and digital processes.

National Scholarship Portal

National Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled. National Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan (NeGP)
Vision-: National Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled. This initiative aims at providing a Simplified, Mission-oriented, Accountable, Responsive & Transparent 'SMART' System for faster & effective disposal of Scholarships applications and delivery of funds directly into beneficiaries account without any leakages.
